What is biochar?

Biochar is made by subjecting organic material to high temperatures in a zero-oxygen environment. This stabilizes carbon for long-term storage and creates a porous, high-surface area material with wide applications.

Benefits

  • Improve crop performance: Enhance microbiome with a proven soil amendment.

  • Sequester carbon: Stabilize biomass carbon through anaerobic pyrolysis. Earn credits to meet emissions goals.

  • Enhance soil structure: Retain water and prevent erosion.
